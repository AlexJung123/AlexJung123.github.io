What makes Prague a "must visit"?

Prague is a beautiful city that you should definitly visit at least once in your life. I went here with my girlfriend and it was such a good time. Prague offers a lot of different sightseeing attractions, like the Carlsbridge, Prague Castle or the John Lennon Wall. It gives you the chance to experience good food and nightlife.

Places to go:

Prague Castle:

The Prague Castle is a historical place to go to in Prague. Did you here of the "Prague window lintel"? You can stand in the room where the thirty years war started in Europe. Beautiful architecture and a historical feeling complete the experience.

Clementinum:

The former Jesuit college surprises the visitor with its size and decorated baroque rooms. At the time of the Jesuits, the extensive baroque complex included the living quarters of the members of the order, school rooms, an observatory, a printing house and three churches which you can visit for an affordable price today.

Swinging Towers & John Lennon Wall:

In 1996, the wave-shaped building on the Vltava River was completed by the arichtecs Vlado Milunić and Frank O. Gehry. At the corner it consists of two leaning and at the same time dynamic towers, which symbolize a dancing couple. The John Lennon Wall is a famous place for beautiful graffiti art in Prague and worth a visit.

Sigmund Freud's Hanging stature & head of Franz Kafka:

When Sigmund Freud's hanging stature was introduced to the public, most people thought of a man doing suicide and reported it to the police. But no worries it is "just" a stature. If you walk trough Prague's streets look up and don't miss this astonishing piece of art. You can find also a moving stature of Franz Kafka's head that changes its shape every full hour.

Carlsbridge:

The Carlsbridge is the famous 516 meters long bridge in Prague which was build by Charlemange in 1357. The best time to enjoy this most beautiful medieval bridge in the world is at dawn, when you have it all to yourself. The Old Town is guarded by the elegant Old Town Bridge Tower, which is one of the most beautiful towers in the world thanks to its stone decorations.

Flea market:

If you are looking for a real czech all day experience, you should visit the prague flee market. This is the craziest flee market I have ever seen in my life. Apart from weapons, old electro machine parts, and a lot of historical stuff you see another side of a society that you won't experience somewhere else.

Bars & Restaurants:
V kralé Brabants:

This is the oldest tavern in Prague. It is the right place to enjoy a special medieval meal and beer. Travel back in time and dive for a short moment into the life people lived hundreds of years ago.

Tiki Taky Bar & Sad Man's Tongue Bar:

A Hawaiian drink in the city of Prague? Yes! Go to this bar to enjoy a well done cocktail. To eat an awesome tasting burger I recommend you to go to Sad Man's Tongue Bar. It's definitly worth it!
