What makes San Diego a "must visit"?

San Diego is a beautiful city at the Californian coast. I spend four months studying in San Diego from september to december and appreciated the time there a lot. You will have good weather even in the fall season what allows you to go out and explore the city and the beaches. I can really recommend to go there during the last quarter of the year to enjoy the christmas time.

Places to go:

The best Beaches:

San Diego provides the visitor with several beaches, each unique on its own. Coronado Beach is next to the Hotel del Coronado and has a wide space of white sand and broad access to the pacific ocean. Mission Beach is a bit smaller than Coronado Beach but much longer and gives the opportunity for a walk on the sand or promenade.

USS Midway:

The USS Midway is an old aircraft carrier that operated for 47 years and was until 1955 the largest ship in the world. It was part of vietnam war and in the Operation Desert Storm. In 1992 it became a museum ship in San Diego's harbor. You can walk through the ship and see different aircrafts on its deck.

Balboa Park:

One place you should definitly visit is Balboa Park. It provides the visitor with great architecture, cultural impressions and history in about 17 museums and nature. Ethnically diversity, historical richness and breathtaking installations make Balboa Park an astonishing experience for the whole family. Especially during christmas time it has a lot of different attractions, such as music events, christmas markets and a lot of delicious food to offer.

Restaurants:
Phil's BBQ:

At Phil's BBQ, you can get delicious experiences due to a combination of the best barbecue and great customer service. Since 1998, BBQ lovers across San Diego County have turned to these restaurants to enjoy a well made meat with some additions, like french fries or oven potatoes. They have been awarded "Best BBQ Catering" in San Diego year after year. I can definitly recommend going there to get a taste of local American food.

Chili's:

If you are searching for a meal that fills your stomach for low effort of money, you are at the right place with Chili's. For only 10 Dollars you can get a three course menu consisting of a starter, a main course and a dessert, as well as, an additional drink. Go to this restaurant to even stop the largest hunger and enjoy some sport matches on Tv.

In & Out:

When first entering In-N-Out you think of entering a McDonald's unless you order your first burger. You will notice that it tastes very different. Only a few people know that In-N-Out has a secret menu which you can only find if you search for it online. On the right picture you see the 4-by-4 burger which speaks for itself.

Sports:
Disc Golf:

Disc Golf is a crazy mixture of golf and frisbee sports. You can rent some frisbees at the entrence of the disc golf course. But be aware that every frisbee has a spezial ability and flies different. Go on one of the disc golf courses and try your best to get to the basket with the lowest amount of throws.

Surfing & Ice Skating:

San Diego is a paradise for surfers. It provides a combination of warm weather and high waves. I started surfing there more spontaneous than prepared. You can go to one of the several surfshops near the beach and rent a board and a swimsuit for affordable prices. Don't be scared of trying it, even I, as a beginner, was able to stand up on the board once or twice. A special advise: In the christmas time you can do ice skating directly at colorado beach.

Ice Hockey & Basketball:

Don't miss the opportunity to watch one of the San Diego Seagulls Ice Hockey Games. There is a great atmosphere created by the hugh amounts of fans supporting their team. Another sport you should follow on at all cost is the american basketball league. Take the bus or car to Los Angeles and watch a game of the LA Lakers. The tickets are definitly worth the price.
