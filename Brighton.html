What makes Brighton a "must visit"?

Brighton is a British city at the Northsea coast that is seen by many Londoners as a great place to spend their vacation. It gives an inclusive, free-thinking city feeling and offers a divers nightlife. With modern culture and exotic architecture, sea and countryside, Brighton is the city to discover new places and get new experiences both inside the city and out.

Places to go:

Beach & Pier:

Brightons beach is a stone based beach which gives the coast a special touch. At the promenade you can find several bars and restaurants lined up and providing people with food and drinks. The Brighton Pier has similarties to the Santa Monica Pier in Los Angles. You can find food and drink offers, a casino and many fun park attractions.

White Cliffs:

The famous White Cliffs of Great Britain, appeared in the Harry Potter movies and are in short distance to Brighton. You can go there by bus and have a nice walk on top or at the bottom of the cliffs. At some days there are even world war like aircrafts flying through the sky, giving a time travler feeling.

Royal Pavillion:

One place you should definitly visit is the Royal Pavillion. The spectacular seaside palace of the Prince Regent (George IV) was transformed between 1815 and 1823 into one of the most exotic buildings on the British Island. The Royal Pavilion mixes Indian architecture and Chinese inspired interiors and provides the visitor with a spectacular garden.

Bars & Clubs:
The Font:

The Font is a great bar specialised on cocktails. Especially during the Happy Hours the atmosphere of this bar is amazing. Go bere to enjoy a nice cocktail in the beginning of the evening.

Ye Olde King & Queen:

Ye Olde King & Queen Bar is a bar that is designed in an old style. They offer a nice atmosphere, have pool tables and show football matches via beamer. Every Tuesday there is a Quiznight where you can sign up for a good evening with some gifts.

PRYZM:

The PRYZM is the heart of Brightons party scene. It started as a cinema with lots of floors and was later redesigned with the purpose to be the largest club in Brighton. They offer three floors with different music and different bars. Every wednesday and at the weekends you can enter PRYZM to enjoy a great party. Top advise: Join the Pub Crawl and you are able to see some different bars in the private area of PRYZM.

Activities:
Pub Crawl:

If you are a student and between 19 to 28 years old the Pub Crawl "On the Rocks" is a must do to experience Brightons divers pub scene. The trip includes around five bars and clubs in total. You will pay an affordable participation fee and get some discounts in every pub you gonna join.

Sushi (all you can eat):

Brighton was the first city in which I tried out eating sushi and it was so delicious. If you are a big sushi fan or did not taste it yet, you should definitly enter one of Brightons sushi restaurant. Search for the one with the best all you can eat offer.

Short Trip to London:

Don't miss the opportunity to get to see Great Britains capital London. From Brighton you can take the train and be in London in less than 45min. In London you can go to different museums, like the nature museum, or go to the royal Buckingham Palace. It's definitly worth it.
